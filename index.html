About Band Advisory

Band Advisory is a special program where students can get extra support with their musical development, practice techniques, and band-related academics. Mrs. Collins works closely with advisory students to help them reach their full potential.


What you can expect:

  • One-on-one practice sessions
  • Music theory tutoring
  • Performance preparation
  • Study hall for band-related coursework
  • Peer mentoring opportunities
